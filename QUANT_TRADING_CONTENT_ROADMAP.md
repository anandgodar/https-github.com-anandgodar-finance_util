# Quantitative Trading Content Roadmap

This document outlines content gaps identified in competitor analysis and provides a roadmap for differentiated quant trading content that can attract high-value traffic.

## Content Gap Analysis

Based on analysis of QuantStart, Quantified Strategies, QuantNet, and Robot Wealth:

| Gap | Opportunity | Difficulty |
|-----|-------------|------------|
| Beginner-friendly quant explanations | High - most content assumes PhD-level math | Medium |
| End-to-end implementation guides | High - fragmented tutorials exist | High |
| Modern tool tutorials (AI/ML) | Very High - rapidly evolving field | Medium |
| Cost-effective retail solutions | High - most focus on institutional | Low |
| Specific market focus (crypto, emerging) | Medium - underserved niches | Medium |
